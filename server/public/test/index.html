Listing With w3bbi

Register

Create an account with w3bbi and register all the domain names you wish to put up for rental. These will not be available to buyers until you list them.

Price

You have full control over the price of your rental based on hourly, weekly, monthly, or even yearly rates. Configure what dates your domain is available for and its respective price.

List!

Once you have made all the appropriate configurations, it's time to list! You will be immediately notified when a seller has placed a bid for your domains.
