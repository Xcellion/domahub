About Us

w3bbi was founded in 2016 to challenge the normal domain name marketplace by creating a platform where domains are rented - not bought - for a reasonable price.

Sellers are now empowered to continously generate income on their domain names while buyers are able to purchase these domains for as long or as short as they please. w3bbi aims to provide significant returns for sellers without exposing buyers to the conventionally exorbitant pricetags associated with domain names.
